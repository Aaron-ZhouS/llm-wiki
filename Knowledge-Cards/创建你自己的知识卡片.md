## Critical: Chinese Text Rendering Rules
To prevent garbled characters, follow these rules strictly

**Character Selection**

- Use ONLY high-frequency Simplified Chinese characters (HSK 1-4 level preferred)
- Avoid rare characters, traditional forms, classical expressions

-Prefer simple strokes: if not i&, not 学,个not 个

Length Limits Per Line*:
